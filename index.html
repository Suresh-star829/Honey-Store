Our Story

Where Every Drop Tells a Story

Founded in 2015 deep in the Nilgiri hills, GoldenHive began with a single idea: honest honey, harvested with respect for bees and nature alike.

Our beekeepers follow traditional methods passed down through generations, ensuring the bees thrive and every jar is pure, potent, and full of life.

  • Directly sourced from certified apiaries across India
  • Cold-extracted to preserve natural enzymes & pollen
  • FSSAI certified & internationally lab-tested
  • Bee-friendly, sustainable farming practices
  • Zero added sugar, no artificial preservatives
